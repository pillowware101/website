Ninja Search is a search engine for the web

Ninja Search was made to give you speedy search results(results may not be speedy now because our server is not completely finished being setup yet).


We do not track your browsing history or sell your browsing data to advertisers.


We use only Raspberry Pis for our server so you can give the Raspberry Pi Foundation that we can bring you

quality search results. You can also thank the author of "The Google Story", David A. Vise because it was his book

that gave me the idea to build this search engine to give search results to the world.
